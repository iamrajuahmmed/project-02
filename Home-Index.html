About Us

7 ONE PROPERTIES LTD. is one of the main concern of 7 ONE GROUP. It is a multi disciplinary organization engaged in multilateral activities related to real estate development, consultancy, and construction since 1998. The mission of this organization is to offer best quality apartments and design high quality homes for the esteemed customers. We have a professional marketing team, group of skilled engineers, designers and management team. Within short span of time, we turned into one of the most trusted and leading developer among the real estate companies.

Customer satisfaction is our main goal. We want to maximize customer satisfaction through quality construction, aesthetic interior, timely delivery, and providing one year after sales service (including six months replacement guarantee of fixture and fittings).

Our tireless effort is to maintain quality services, mainly fueled by two streams quality materials and expert workmanship. We are dedicated to utilize sophisticated materials. Moreover, we assure a high standard of expert services at every stage of construction.

THE STORYLINES OF HAPPINES

Seven One Group was establishd in 2001 with the aim of touching and enhancing all angels of life for the consumers with influential brands, reliable products and consistent service. It is one of the fastest growing conglomerates in Bangladesh. We have a strong and innovative culture and we maintain the aesthetic design through our creation. With the primary aim to fulfill client satisfaction through total quality control, design excellence and experience. We continuously work on the diversification of products and services.

We are also blessed to have robust and decisive leadership. Seven One Group’s journey of 22 years has been glorious and through this journey, we have fulfilled the dreams of a lot of people by constructing apartments and commercial projects in Bangladesh. We have successfully handed 60 projects, 800 units and 10,00000 square feet. This shows our dedication and sincerity to the real estate industry. We look forward to creating more so that the Seven One Group continues to create history in the Bangladesh real estate sector.
